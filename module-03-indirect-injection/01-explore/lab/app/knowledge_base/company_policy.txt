# Meridian Technologies — Employee Policy Handbook

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Core collaboration hours are 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM in the employee's local timezone.
Remote workers must maintain a reliable internet connection and be reachable via Slack.

## Expense Policy
Business expenses over $500 require VP-level pre-approval.
Travel bookings must be made through the approved corporate travel portal.
Meal reimbursement is capped at $75/day for domestic travel and $100/day internationally.

## Security Policy
All employees must complete annual security awareness training by Q4.
Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all internal systems.
Sensitive data must be classified according to the Data Classification Framework:
  - PUBLIC: Marketing materials, published blog posts
  - INTERNAL: Strategy documents, meeting notes, roadmaps
  - CONFIDENTIAL: Customer data, financial records, API keys
  - RESTRICTED: Encryption keys, security audit results

Report security incidents to security@meridiantech.com within 24 hours.

## PTO Policy
Full-time employees accrue 20 days PTO per calendar year.
Unused PTO carries over up to a maximum of 5 days.
PTO requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance for absences > 3 days.
