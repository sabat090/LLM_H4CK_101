# Meridian Technologies — Q3 2025 Quarterly Business Review

## Executive Summary
Q3 showed strong growth across all business units. Revenue exceeded
projections by 12%, driven primarily by enterprise deal closures.
Customer churn decreased to 3.2%, the lowest in company history.

## Financial Highlights
- Revenue: $8.2M (vs $7.3M projected)
- Gross Margin: 78%
- Operating Expenses: $6.1M
- Net Income: $1.4M
- Cash Position: $22.5M

## Product Updates
- DocuBot v2.5 shipped with improved search and summarization
- Customer satisfaction for DocuBot: 4.3/5.0
- API response times improved 40% after infrastructure migration

## Key Wins
- Signed 3 enterprise contracts worth combined $4.2M ARR
- Achieved ISO 27001 certification
- Launched in European market with first 12 customers

## Challenges
- Engineering hiring slower than planned (8 hires vs 12 target)
- Mobile app launch delayed to Q1 2026
- Two P1 security incidents required post-mortem reviews

## Action Items
- Accelerate engineering hiring pipeline (VP Engineering)
- Complete mobile app beta by December 2025 (Product Lead)
- Implement enhanced monitoring for production systems (DevOps)
